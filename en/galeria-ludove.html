Folk Embroideries and Garments.

Folk costumes, shirts, lajblíks and vests from the estate of the Šenšel family. We cannot always say for certain where each piece came from or what it was used for — but some of them were likely connected to the work of the Lipa cooperative.

Tamara Šimončíková Heribanová, Stanislav Talapka, Zuzana Tajek Piešová and Michal Halabrin generously helped me with identification. Given the condition and state in which the pieces were found, we have tried to identify them as precisely as possible. If you happen to know anything more, I would be glad if you got in touch — every detail helps.
