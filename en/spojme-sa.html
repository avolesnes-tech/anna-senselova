Your story
has worth.

"This website was born out of love and reverence for the heritage of my ancestors."

Two carrier bags found in my grandparents' cellar led me to a story I couldn't let slip into forgetting. Embroideries, dolls, old issues of Živena — and behind them, an entire world that nearly vanished. Anna Šenšelová, Lipa, tens of thousands of hands keeping the beauty of folk art alive. All of this deserves to go on living.

Created in my spare time

This website was not made to order, and certainly not out of duty. It came to life because I loved it — searching, reading, piecing together a story from fragments that had been shut away in a carrier bag for decades. It was a genuine joy, and I wanted to share it.

If you have your own story that deserves to be told — a family history, folk art, a place that must not be forgotten — and you'd like to give it a lasting digital home, please do reach out. I'd love to talk about what you have and what might be possible. No strings attached.
